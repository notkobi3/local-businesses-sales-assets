## Recommended Pricing Framework

These numbers are intentionally positioned below typical agency pricing and within a realistic freelancer range for small local businesses.

### Starter
- Best for: solo operators, salons, simple local businesses
- Typical scope: 3 to 4 pages, mobile responsive, contact section, map, gallery, simple SEO basics
- Recommended price: $700 to $1,100

### Starter Plus
- Best for: local restaurants and established small storefronts
- Typical scope: 4 to 5 pages, menu or services section, gallery, directions, stronger branding
- Recommended price: $900 to $1,600

### Growth
- Best for: service businesses that need leads
- Typical scope: 4 to 6 pages, stronger conversion layout, service-area messaging, forms, call-first mobile design
- Recommended price: $1,100 to $1,900

## What You Should Charge

If you are still building your client base, I would pitch these businesses in the following range:
- Entry offer: $850 to $1,200 for most restaurant or storefront sites
- Strong service-business offer: $1,300 to $1,500 for towing, tree service, or lead-gen style sites
- Monthly care plan if they want updates: $40 to $100 per month

My practical recommendation:
- Charge Jeff's Hair Design: about $850
