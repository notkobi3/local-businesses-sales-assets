# Client Pricing Options

Last updated: March 29, 2026

This document is a simple pricing sheet for offering website services to local businesses.

## Recommended Website Payment Structure

For most small business website projects:
- 50% down payment before work starts
- 50% due when the website is ready to launch
- Optional monthly support plan after launch

Example:
- Website build: $1,200
- Deposit: $600 to begin
- Final payment: $600 at delivery

Why this structure works:
- It protects your time
- It filters out non-serious clients
- It gives the client a clear and familiar payment plan
- It keeps monthly billing focused on real ongoing value

## Monthly Plan Options

These plans are meant to be simple, easy to explain, and affordable for local businesses.

### 1. Basic Care Plan
- Price: $49/month
- Best for: businesses that rarely update their site
- Includes:
  - up to 2 small content updates per month
  - contact info or hours updates
  - image swaps
  - basic site check to make sure pages are loading
  - email support for minor issues

### 2. Standard Support Plan
- Price: $89/month
- Best for: restaurants, salons, and storefronts that need regular edits
- Includes:
  - up to 5 content updates per month
  - menu, service, or pricing updates
  - photo changes
  - homepage banner or seasonal promotion swaps
  - monthly check for broken links or display issues
  - priority email support

### 3. Growth Plan
- Price: $149/month
- Best for: service businesses that want a more active website presence
- Includes:
  - up to 8 content updates per month
  - new service section or landing page adjustments
  - stronger homepage callout updates
  - lead form review
  - monthly content refresh suggestions
  - priority support

## Optional Add-On Services

These can be offered separately when a client does not want a larger monthly plan.

### One-Time Update Fee
- Price: $40 to $100 per request
- Use for: small edits when the client is not on a monthly plan

### Additional Landing Page
- Price: $150 to $300
- Use for: adding a new services page, event page, or promotional page

### Rush Update
- Price: $50 to $100 extra
- Use for: same-day or next-day changes

## Recommended Pitch

The easiest structure to sell is:
- one-time website build
- 50% upfront
- optional monthly support after launch

That keeps the offer simple and avoids scaring off smaller local businesses with a mandatory recurring charge.

## Suggested Pricing Language

You can say:

"For the website build, I usually do a 50% deposit up front and the remaining 50% when the site is ready to launch. After that, I offer optional monthly support plans if you want help with updates, changes, or keeping the site current."

## Best Starting Offer

If you want to keep things simple early on:
- Website build: $900 to $1,500 depending on the business
- Deposit: 50% upfront
- Monthly option 1: $49/month
- Monthly option 2: $89/month
- Monthly option 3: $149/month

## Notes By Business Type

### Restaurants
- Best monthly fit: Basic or Standard
- Common updates:
  - hours
  - menu prices
  - specials
  - event banners

### Salons and Barbershops
- Best monthly fit: Basic or Standard
- Common updates:
  - services
  - pricing
  - staff info
  - gallery photos

### Towing, Auto, Tree Service, Home Improvement
- Best monthly fit: Standard or Growth
- Common updates:
  - service area
  - promotions
  - service pages
  - lead-focused homepage changes
